AHAU is the first Turkish company to produce shock absorber units for speedboats and boats. Understanding all the danger to which the passenger's spine is exposed during the jumping of the ship into the waves, we want the opportunity to make water travel comfortable and safe to be available to everyone. Therefore, our goal is to offer products that meet high international quality standards to the Turkish Sunday at an affordable price.
Teaming up with a team of the best engineers, designers and technologists, we Deconstructed the expertise of foreign manufacturers and adapted it to the needs of our customers. When working with partners, AHAU demonstrates the maximum flexibility that makes it possible to design and manufacture shelves specifically. For more information, contact us!
Our staff carefully prepares the products for transportation to eliminate the possibility of damage. We make delivery free of charge within the borders of Turkey.
AHAU shock absorber units are designed for various seat types. They have stepless height adjustment, which allows anyone to quickly set up a shelf on their own. The degree of rigidity of the shock absorber can also be adjusted. Our racks are designed for heavy loads and a wide range of weights are on the seat.
The basis of Ahau's production is careful monitoring of each process, from the purchase of materials to the packaging of the finished product, which allows us to guarantee the reliability and durability of products. If within 1 year any defects appear on the purchased product that are not caused by a violation of the rules of use, transportation and storage, we undertake to replace the product with a similar model.
